DIAGRAM: Service-to-Service Patterns
PURPOSE: Shows the five concerns of synchronous service-to-service communication (discovery, authentication, cross-VPC access, traffic management, observability) plus asynchronous patterns (EventBridge, Step Functions). Organized as architectural concerns, not service choices.
LAYOUT: top-to-bottom
WIDTH: 700px maximum readable width

GROUPS:

- Asynchronous (event-driven): border style dashed, color purple (#7c3aed); contains Events
