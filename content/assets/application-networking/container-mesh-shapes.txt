DIAGRAM: Container Mesh Shapes
PURPOSE: Shows the three shapes of container service-to-service architecture on AWS: (1) In-cluster container networking (EKS, ECS), (2) Amazon VPC Lattice as an alternative to a mesh, and (3) Self-managed mesh on AWS networking. Helps readers decide which shape fits their requirements.

- Self-managed mesh on AWS networking: border style dashed, color green (#059669)
